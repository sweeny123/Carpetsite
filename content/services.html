Services

  Whether it's feel or functionality you're looking for, you won't have to compromise when it comes to selecting a product for your home or business. At Wagner Carpets, we specialize in helping you find exactly what you're looking for even if you're not quite sure what that is. We take several factors into account before recommending any products for your living space - including, the use of the space, the amount of foot traffic, pets, sunlight and temperature exposure, etc. We offer free onsite estimates with samples brought right to your home or business. This allows you to compare the products and colors with your own decor and get a better feel for what you are looking for. We also have a showroom with many more options to choose from. So whether it be guestroom or dining room, kitchen or sun room, office space or retail, home gym or daycare, we'll help you find the highest quality product perfect for your living space and your budget.
