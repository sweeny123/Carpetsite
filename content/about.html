About Us

Andy Wagner was born and raised in the Rochester area and with that comes a tremendous love for the City. He began his journey as a Professional Carpet Installer in 2002 and became an Independent Installer in 2012. Continuing to install for the various carpet stores in Rochester, Andy quickly became known as the go-to installer for high-end and difficult jobs. With an eye for detail and a drive for excellence that is unsurpassed in the industry, Andy has done everything from cottages and apartments to businesses and multi-million dollar homes. Installing all over the city and into parts of Ontario and the Finger Lakes, Andy's success as an installer eventually lead him to adding an assistant installer to his one-man team in 2015.

When approached by a fellow carpet installer and asked if Andy would be interested in buying his carpet business from him, it seemed like the next logical step and in 2018, Wagner Carpets was born. Throughout his many years as an installer, Andy has gotten to know which carpets work best in which areas. So owning his own store has created a very unique opportunity for him to bring this extensive knowledge and understanding to the sales floor. You will find that Andy specializes in matching you with product samples that will not only meet your needs but will also fit your budget and look beautiful for many years to come. That's the awesome thing about Andy, when the sales person is the installer - you know you can trust the quality of the product you are choosing.

With more business comes more hands. Andy has not only added to his team of installers but is also working with a group of excellent guys who install all of the hard surface flooring jobs. Built on honesty, integrity and a love for this City, Wagner Carpets is proud to serve both Rochester and the surrounding areas.
