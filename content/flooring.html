Flooring

Flooring is much like carpet in that it helps define the feel of a room. And with the various types of hard surface flooring on the market today and the differing wear layers, it can be a bit daunting trying to decide what's right for you.

At Wagner Carpets, we help to simplify the situation by taking the time to get to know not only the type of room (you and the area) you're shopping for but also the feel you want it to have. We'll evaluate the amount of foot traffic it will see, if there will be any temperature changes or sun exposure, whether there will be pets or children and, of course, the possibility of moisture or spills. This is just to name a few. Then we will assist you in choosing the right type of flooring suitable not only for the use of the space but also for your budget. A floor that will fit the look and feel you want with a durability that will last for many years to come.

So whether it's the appearance of ceramic or stone that you want, or the look of hardwood - or even if you're just not sure - we'll be there to help throughout the decision process. And with our wide array of luxury vinyl tile and luxury vinyl planks, laminate, hardwood and waterproof flooring, you can be sure we have the perfect selection for any room anywhere. From kitchens and baths to hallways and mudrooms, home gyms and basements to corporate offices and retail spaces, we have everything you need for home or business.
